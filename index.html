About Me

As a dedicated Software Engineer, my core expertise lies in JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and web development with React. I excel in transforming technical requirements into high-quality, user-centric web solutions, ensuring both functionality and an exceptional user experience.

In the blockchain sector, I am engaged as a Developer Advocate at the Casper Association. My role encompasses not only the advocacy of the association's initiatives but also hands-on involvement in developing blockchain solutions. My experience includes writing smart contracts and constructing decentralized applications (dApps), contributing to the practical evolution of blockchain technology.

Alongside my core skills, I possess additional experience with Python, Swift, Rust, SQL, and Linux systems, among others. This varied technical background enhances my versatility in tackling diverse programming challenges. My skill set is further complemented by proficiency in the Adobe Creative Suite, such as Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, and Premiere Pro, which aids in delivering creative and visually appealing digital solutions.
